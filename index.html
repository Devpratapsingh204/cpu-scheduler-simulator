Algorithm Reference

FCFS
First Come First Served. Non-preemptive. Processes run in arrival order. Simple but may cause the convoy effect with varied burst times.
Non-Preemptive
SJF Non-Preemptive
Shortest Job First. Selects the process with the smallest burst time from the ready queue. Optimal average waiting time.
Non-Preemptive
SRTF
Shortest Remaining Time First. Preemptive version of SJF. Theoretically optimal for average waiting time. Higher context-switch overhead.
Preemptive
Round Robin
Each process receives a fixed time quantum in cyclic order. Fair allocation. Higher quantum converges to FCFS behavior.
Preemptive · Time-sliced
Priority Non-Preemptive
Highest priority (lowest number) runs to completion first. Can cause starvation of low priority processes without aging mechanisms.
Non-Preemptive
Priority Preemptive
Running process is preempted when a higher-priority process arrives. More responsive for urgent tasks. Starvation risk without aging.
Preemptive
